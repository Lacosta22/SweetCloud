Easy~Peasy Recipe

  • 4 cups - Sugar
  • 1 cup - corn syrup
  • 1 cup - water
  • ¼ tsp. - salt
  • 1 tbsp - flavor extract (your choice of flavor)
  • 2 drops food coloring (your color choice
  • Lollipop sticks for serving

Step 1: Combine the Ingredients - 4 cups of sugar, 1 cup of corn syrup, 1 cup of water, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt in saucepan over medium high heat, stir ingredients together. Once sugar is melted make sure to wipe down the sides of the pan to avoid sugar crystals from forming.

Step 2: Place a candy thermometer in the saucepan. Heat mixture to 320 degrees F. Once the mixture has reached that temperature, pour the hot liquid into a heatproof container. Add the extract and food coloring, stir the ingredients well.

Step 3: Make sure to lay parchment paper on the countertop or area you plan to spin your cotton candy on.

Step 4: Dip your whisk into the sugar syrup. Hold it over the pot and allow the sugar to drip back into the container for just a second. Hold it about 10 to 12 inches above the parchment and swing it back and forth. Allow thin strands of sugar to fall on the parchment paper.Continue doing this until you've got a nice size of spun sugar. This version of cotton candy tends to be a bit messier than the cotton candy we buy at carnivals or that is spun in a cotton candy spinning machine. But the taste is the same and it is a fun way to get your little (and big) kids involved. Have fun!

Step 5: You can spin it on a lollipop stick or place it in a bowl to enjoy. Be sure not to allow the spun sugar to brittle.
